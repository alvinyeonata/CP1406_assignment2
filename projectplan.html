Diazepam Cafe Project Plan

Mission Statement

This website is intended to promote Diazepam Cafe. The goal is to be the best ice-cream and desserts cafe in Singapore by producing them with the highest quaility of natural ingredients.

Success Evaluation

There will be a landslide of customers coming to the cafe during the events. There will be an announcement of when the event will be held and what it's about. There will also be a brief information about the product.

Target Audience

 This website is for all-age catagory but especially created for ice-cream & dessert lovers. The website is made so that the viewer will be captivated by the images of the product.

Content

  1. Main Page
  2. Products
  3. Events
  4. Service Page
  5. Online order form
  6. Online feedback form
